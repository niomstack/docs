**What if your computer could think ahead?**

NIOM is an always-on AI agent that lives on your desktop. It watches your workspace, understands context, and takes action proactively — before you ask. No cloud dependencies. No data leaving your machine. Just a quiet daemon that makes your computer genuinely useful.

<Note>
  NIOM is in active development (alpha). The architecture is stable and new features ship weekly.
</Note>

## Under the hood

```
You work → NIOM watches → Understands context → Takes action → You approve (or it handles it)
```

NIOM runs as two lightweight processes:

1. **Rust (Tauri)** — Thin OS shell (~200 lines). System tray, hotkeys, notifications.
2. **Node.js Sidecar** — The brain. AI SDK v6, agent engine, tools, MCP, streaming.

Press `Ctrl+Space` to summon the overlay. The daemon runs silently in the background.
